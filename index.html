I was walking alone on the misty outskirts of Preston when the sky shimmered with colors I’d never seen. A silver craft descended silently, and a hatch hissed open. Small beings with translucent, glowing skin stepped out.

They didn’t speak, but I understood them. They weren’t here to harm us—they wanted to show me how fragile our world is, how every choice matters.

Then they were gone, leaving only a chocolate bar.
